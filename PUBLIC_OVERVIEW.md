# Public Product Overview

## Mission

Alpha Ghost turns fragmented market information into a single, explainable operating decision while preserving human oversight and strict risk controls.

## High-Level Workflow

1. **Perceive** — ingest live market, technical, portfolio, and catalyst data.
2. **Interpret** — evaluate trend, volatility, liquidity, momentum, and risk.
3. **Challenge** — test competing explanations and reject contradictory conclusions.
4. **Decide** — publish one unified operator-facing decision.
5. **Act** — route approved instructions to the appropriate execution environment.
6. **Learn** — record outcomes and update future decision context.

## Publicly Disclosed Capabilities

- market-regime classification;
- technical and contextual market analysis;
- structured, explainable decision outputs;
- risk and exposure monitoring;
- operator dashboards and event logs;
- integration with external market-data and trading services.

## Intentionally Undisclosed

The following remain private:

- source code and container topology;
- internal prompts and model-routing logic;
- signal weighting and confidence calculations;
- position-sizing and execution algorithms;
- proprietary decision-admission rules;
- infrastructure credentials and operational runbooks.

This document is descriptive only and does not provide enough information to reproduce the Alpha Ghost implementation.
