# Alpha Ghost — Autonomous Trading Intelligence

![Alpha Ghost cover](assets/alpha-ghost-cover.png)

**Alpha Ghost is a private, locally hosted trading-intelligence platform developed by Jonathan Wells through Fortified Capital LLC.** It combines live market data, AI-assisted reasoning, risk controls, and operator-facing dashboards to support fast, explainable trading decisions.

> **Show the magic. Don’t reveal the magic trick.**

## What Alpha Ghost Demonstrates

- Real-time market analysis and regime detection
- Structured AI reasoning and explainable decision outputs
- Risk-aware trade planning and execution support
- Persistent operational context across specialized services
- A unified operator cockpit for monitoring system state, opportunities, and risk

## The Organism Concept

Alpha Ghost is designed as an **organism-inspired intelligence system** rather than a loose collection of disconnected agents. Specialized components perceive, evaluate, remember, challenge, and coordinate through a unified decision framework.

This public repository intentionally describes the product at a high level only. It does **not** contain proprietary source code, prompts, algorithms, execution logic, infrastructure credentials, or internal architecture.

## Project Status

- **Deployment:** Private, locally hosted
- **Public demo:** Not currently available
- **Source code:** Proprietary and maintained in a private repository
- **Public materials:** Product overview, brand assets, and showcase imagery
